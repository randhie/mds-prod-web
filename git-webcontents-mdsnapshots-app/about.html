About Randy

Biography

After many years as a keen professional photographer, I decided to start a small landscape and portrait photography business called MDSnapShots which I ran alongside my main job as an IT Software Developer. As business increased, I found myself working long, but rewarding, hours just to keep up with the demand. It quickly became apparent that I was enjoying both my part time and full time job.

If you like what you see and would like more information - let's chat. We would love to learn more about you and your photo shoot motives and plan.
