Corporate portrait photography

Corporate portrait photography needs vary from coming in to an office to take a single portrait, to doing a complete office of 50+ staff or more. Accordingly prices vary, but significant discounts are available for volume as the time for set-up and take down remains the same regardless of the number of shots taken. Please contact me to request a written quote. For your information, please review the following bullet points about my corporate portrait photography service applicable to all clients:

  • The minimum amount of time needed for a good family portrait session is 2hrs (whether you are a family of 2 or 20). This time includes assessing optimal set up areas (if the photos are to be taken in your home), lighting set-ups, and some "getting to know you" time particularly important for families with young children. The creation of a family portrait is the creation of a family and as such deserves some special attention. Part of my work as a family portrait photographer is to capture the essence and uniqueness of your specific family in a specific moment in time which means we need a little time to get to know each other.
  • A typical family portrait session starts at $100
  • A typical family portrait session can yield from between 50-250+ photos to choose from
  • Photos are shot in full colour but can be delivered in either colour or black and white (editing fees apply for black and white delivery)
  • All (or a selection made by the client) unedited images are also available for delivery by password protected download at high resolution (large JPEG files) upon client request at no additional charge.
  • Photos can be all view thru my website and required user name and password to get access.
